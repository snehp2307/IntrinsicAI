Global Feature Importance

GradientBoosting MDI + Permutation Importance across 5,000 companies
Computing importances…
Methodology: A GradientBoostingRegressor (200 estimators, depth=4) is trained on the 5,000-company dataset using 9 financial ratios to predict risk scores. MDI importance measures average impurity reduction per feature. Permutation importance measures score degradation when a feature is randomly shuffled — more reliable but slower. Final ranking uses a 50/50 ensemble of both methods.
